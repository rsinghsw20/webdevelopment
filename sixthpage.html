Scholarship Description

The Ontario Graduate Engineering Excellence Scholarship is designed to attract top-tier international students to pursue Master's or PhD level research in Engineering disciplines within the province of Ontario. This prestigious award recognizes academic merit, research potential, and leadership qualities. Scholars will join a vibrant academic community and gain access to state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and industry partnerships across the Canadian technological corridor.

Eligibility Criteria

  • check_circle Must hold an undergraduate degree in Engineering or closely related technical field with a minimum 8.5/10 CGPA.
  • check_circle Valid English proficiency score (IELTS: 7.5+ or TOEFL: 100+).
  • check_circle Proof of admission or application to an accredited Ontario public university.
  • check_circle Applicants must be international students (Non-Canadian citizens or permanent residents).
